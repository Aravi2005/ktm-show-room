About the shop

This is the KTM Bike collections.

KTM is an Austrian motorcycle, bicycle, and motorsports brand co-owned by Indian manufacturer Bajaj Auto and Austrian manufacturer Pierer Mobility AG. It was founded in 1934 as Kronreif & Trunkenpolz Mattighofen. KTM motorcycles are developed with a philosophy of being READY TO RACE, requiring no modifications to line up on the starting grid.
